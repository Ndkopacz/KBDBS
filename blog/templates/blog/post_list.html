About us

Kopacz-Boyer Design Build Services, LLC (KBDBS, LLC), a Service-Disabled Veteran Owned Small Business, has been in operation since October 2015. KBDBS, LLC is a full service company offering design/build, engineering, electrical contracting, general contracting and construction management services. The principle members of the organization together have over 80 years of experience with Federal government and design/build projects. We know how to meet cost, schedule and performance requirements. Maintaining our reputation for timeliness, quality and professionalism is our priority.

We have Technical Expertise and Experience. KBDBS owners have won, mobilized and/or managed numerous successful IDIQ contracts (SABERs, SATOCs, MATOCs, MACCs, JOCs) including Cheyenne Mountain AFB, Schriever AFB, Peterson AFB, Ft. Carson Army Post, United States Air Force Academy, Buckley AFB, FE Warren AFB, and Cannon AFB.

Project management also includes projects outside the continental US. KBDBS personnel includes a General Contractor (Commercial), registered Professional Engineer (electrical), Master Electrician/Electrical Contractor, Project Management Professional (PMP), LEED GA, and MBA. Staff also skilled in estimating software, scheduling software, computer aided drafting, specification writing, and contract management.

We have a full complement of qualified subcontractors to augment our in-house capabilities, enabling us to meet all contract requirements.

Services

236220 - Commercial and Institutional Buildings

Our team of highly qualified professionals provides expertise in general contracting and construction management. We provide professional engineering services with over 30 cumulative years of experience in LEED Compliant Construction.
