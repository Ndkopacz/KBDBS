About us

Kopacz-Boyer Design Build Services, LLC (KBDBS, LLC), a Service-Disabled Veteran Owned Small Business, has been in operation since October 2015. KBDBS, LLC is a full service company offering design/build, engineering, electrical contracting, general contracting and construction management services. The principle members of the organization together have over 80 years of experience with Federal government and design/build projects. We know how to meet cost, schedule and performance requirements. Maintaining our reputation for timeliness, quality, and professionalism is our priority.

Core Values: KBDBS is committed to helping our clients achieve their goals while delivering the highest quality project attainable and maintaining rigorous business and ethical standards.
